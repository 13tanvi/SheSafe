# 🛡️ SheSafe – Women Safety Platform

SheSafe is a modern web platform designed to promote women’s safety and empowerment by providing quick access to emergency support, awareness resources, and reporting tools.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 🚨 **SOS Emergency Button** for instant alert simulation
* 📞 **Helpline Numbers** (112, 1091, 181)
* ⚖️ **Women Issues Section** with guidance and solutions
* 📝 **Report Issue Form** (user-friendly interface)
* 💡 **Safety Tips & Awareness** resources

---

## 🧠 Project Objective

The goal of SheSafe is to address real-world safety challenges faced by women by offering a simple, accessible, and user-friendly digital solution. This project follows the **Design Thinking approach**:

* Identify the problem
* Design a solution
* Provide actionable support
